Art. 10

International Trade and Customs Law

Legal support for companies involved in import-export, international transactions and customs operations.

Main services

  • Advice on international trade regulations.
  • Interpretation and application of rules of origin, trade restrictions, embargoes and international sanctions.
  • Drafting and negotiating international commercial contracts.
  • Shipping and transport documentation: letters of credit, insurance policies, certificates of origin, customs documents.
  • Representation before the customs authorities of the Republic of Moldova, at the administrative stage and in customs-law disputes.
  • Customs authorisations and licences, including suspensive or temporary-storage customs regimes.
  • Challenging customs decisions: duty recalculations, refusal to release goods, unjustified sanctions.
  • Seizure of goods and customs-fraud allegations.
  • Tax legislation applicable to international trade, including special VAT regimes.
  • Compliance with EU legislation on foreign trade.

We help clients minimise legal risk, avoid penalties and speed up import and export processes.
