Art. 17

Real Estate and Construction Law

Specialist legal advice and full representation in real-estate transactions and construction projects.

Our services include

  • Real-estate transactions: sale-purchase, lease, real-estate leasing, concession agreements — drafting, review and negotiation, including mortgages, easements and security in rem.
  • Real-estate development projects: support for investors, developers and contractors in obtaining building permits, zoning certificates and other approvals; construction contracts, cost-sharing, termination or dealings with subcontractors.
  • Real-estate due diligence: review of a property's legal history, checking for risks, litigation, encumbrances and restrictions; assessment of tax, zoning and cadastral status.
  • Real-estate litigation: representation on ownership, division of property, easements, eviction, enforcement or claims; challenging zoning fines or sanctions.
