Art. 20

Defence of Honour, Dignity and Professional Reputation

Specialist legal assistance to protect the image, honour and reputation of clients, whether individuals or companies.

Representation in defamation disputes

  • Insult, slander, disparagement, attacks in the press or on social media
  • Defence against offensive articles, false reviews, unfounded accusations or media harassment campaigns

Remedies for the harm suffered

  • Compensation for moral and material damages suffered
  • Removal or deletion of defamatory content, online and offline
  • Orders to publish apologies or official corrections

Out-of-court action and negotiation

  • Formal notices and requests to publications, websites, social networks or media platforms
  • Strategies to limit the negative impact on public image
  • Rapid intervention to stop the spread of defamatory information

We work with public figures, professionals and companies affected by unjustified attacks, as well as victims of online defamation.
