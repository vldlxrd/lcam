Art. 01

Banking and Finance, Capital Markets

Full legal consultancy in banking and capital markets, for financial institutions, companies, investors and investment funds.

Main services provided

  • Advice on all types of financial transactions, including syndicated loans, bond issues, financial leasing, factoring, forfaiting and other corporate financing mechanisms.
  • Debt restructuring and loan refinancing, for both banking institutions and corporate debtors.
  • Drafting and negotiating loan agreements, security agreements (mortgages, pledges, personal guarantees, bank guarantee letters) and financing-related documentation.
  • Preparing legal opinions on the validity, legality and enforceability of the legal instruments used in financings.
  • Legal support for the issuance of securities — shares, bonds, structured instruments or other financial products.
  • Assistance with registration procedures before the National Commission for Financial Markets.
  • Representation in dealings with the National Bank of Moldova (NBM), including authorization of financial institutions, licence amendments and compliance with prudential requirements.
  • Defence before the financial regulator, in cases of inspections, investigations or sanctions.
  • Support in mergers and acquisitions in the banking and financial sector, including legal due diligence and compliance opinions.

With a pragmatic, results-oriented approach, we become the trusted partner for companies and institutions active in banking, finance or capital markets.
