Art. 07

Commercial Transactions and Corporate Finance

Specialist legal advice for commercial transactions, complex financing structures and investments.

Our services include

  • Drafting, reviewing and negotiating commercial documentation for the acquisition and sale of assets, shares or equity interests, lending transactions, capital increases, leasing, factoring, mezzanine financing and other financial instruments.
  • Structuring transactions in cooperation with financial advisers, for optimal commercial and tax outcomes.
  • Creating and enforcing security (mortgages, chattel pledges, sureties, personal or in-rem guarantees).
  • Managing contractual risk, by removing clauses that could cause problems long-term.
  • Cross-border financings or those with an international component, in line with regulatory standards.

Whatever the complexity of the transaction or the sector involved, we provide efficient, results-oriented legal services.
