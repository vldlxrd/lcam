Art. 05

Competition Law

Specialist legal advice on competition and antitrust law, to help you avoid legal and financial risk.

Our services include

  • Review of commercial contracts to identify anti-competitive clauses (exclusivity, price-fixing, market allocation).
  • Advice on abuse of dominant position: excessive pricing, discriminatory practices, unjustified refusal to supply.
  • Clearance of economic concentrations (mergers, acquisitions), including notification to and clearance from the Competition Council.
  • Representation in antitrust investigations, hearings, inquiries and administrative or judicial proceedings.
  • Drafting and filing complaints against competitors engaging in anti-competitive practices.
  • State-aid regime, including cases of unlawful aid or recovery.
  • Internal compliance programmes to prevent future sanctions.
  • Legal training for management and staff on good competition practice.

Whether you want to prevent a breach of competition law or are already the subject of an investigation, we provide the support you need to protect your interests and your reputation in the market.
