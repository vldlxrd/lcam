Art. 11

Privatisation, Public Procurement and Public-Sector Assets

Complete legal assistance for investors and companies taking part in privatisation, public-procurement or concession procedures.

Our services include

  • Advice on privatisation, procurement or concession processes for public goods and services — state enterprises, state or local-authority assets, public or private-domain property.
  • Documentation for selection procedures: participation files, letters of intent and bids, challenges to tender results.
  • Relations with public authorities: public-tender procedures, contract negotiations, post-award inspections.
  • Legal regime of public and private assets: rights and obligations arising from privatisation or concession contracts, restrictions on the use and transfer of assets.

Why work with us

  • We know privatisation, concession and public-procurement law in detail
  • We offer practical solutions to maximise your chances of success
  • We help investors secure the best terms of access to public assets
  • We protect clients' interests during the transaction and afterwards
