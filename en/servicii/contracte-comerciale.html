Art. 03

Commercial Contracts

Drafting, review and negotiation of all types of commercial contracts, regardless of complexity or field.

What we deliver, concretely

  • Drafting commercial contracts from scratch, tailored to each client — from start-ups to multinational corporations.
  • Reviewing existing contracts, identifying hidden risks, abusive clauses or terms that do not comply with the law.
  • Negotiating contracts to secure the best terms, conditions and guarantees.
  • Adapting contracts to legislative changes, including for business relationships already under way.
  • Managing contractual risk: liability limitation clauses, penalties, clear deadlines, dispute-resolution mechanisms.
  • Annexes, addenda and general or special contracting terms.
  • Assistance with termination, renegotiation or contractual disputes, including representation in court or arbitration.

Contract types we specialise in

  • Supply and distribution agreements
  • Franchise agreements
  • Operating and financial leasing agreements
  • Service agreements
  • Agency, exclusivity or non-compete agreements
  • Cooperation agreements, strategic partnerships and memoranda of understanding (MoU)
  • General terms and conditions of sale or purchase
  • International commercial contracts, in line with INCOTERMS or applicable conventions

With Legal Corporate Assist you get more than a contract — a real legal guarantee for your business.
