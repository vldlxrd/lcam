Art. 14

Administrative Litigation

Complete legal assistance in disputes against public authorities, under the Administrative Code.

Our services include

  • Court representation: defending clients' interests against public authorities or regulators — annulment of an unfavourable administrative act, compelling the issuance of a favourable act, suspension of an act's enforcement.
  • Drafting procedural documents: challenging acts in the preliminary procedure, annulment applications, actions to have a right recognised or to compel the issuance of an act.
  • Legal advice and strategy: assistance in dealings with administrative authorities and guidance towards an amicable resolution wherever possible.

Areas covered

  • Disputes over duties and taxes
  • Disputes relating to zoning regulations and building permits
  • Issues concerning operating authorisations and licences
  • Disputes over special authorisation regimes
