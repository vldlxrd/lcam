Art. 12

Project Finance and Public-Private Partnership

Full legal support for infrastructure, energy, transport and public-utility projects financed through PPP or project finance.

Our services cover every stage of the project

  • Legal structuring of the project: choosing the partnership model, identifying risks, setting up dedicated special-purpose vehicles (SPVs).
  • Key documentation: concession, PPP or joint-venture agreements, financing and security agreements, addenda.
  • Compliance with national and EU legislation: state aid, public procurement, competition, international standards (EIB, EBRD).
  • Relations with the authorities and institutions involved: obtaining the necessary approvals, licences and clearances.
  • Support throughout the life of the project: monitoring implementation, managing disputes and renegotiations.

Advantages of working with us

  • Multidisciplinary expertise in large-scale, highly complex projects
  • An integrated approach — legal, financial and commercial
  • Flexibility and solutions tailored to each partner, public or private
  • Transparency and rigour at every stage
