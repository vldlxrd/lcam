Art. 04

Bankruptcy and Insolvency

Complete legal assistance at every stage of insolvency prevention and management procedures.

Our services include

  • Insolvency prevention: restructuring plans, amicable solutions, preventive arrangements and other recovery mechanisms.
  • Drafting and filing petitions to open insolvency proceedings, as debtor or creditor.
  • Reorganisation plans to restore the business and avoid bankruptcy.
  • Court representation at every stage: opening, conduct, reorganisation, bankruptcy or liquidation.
  • Representation on creditors' committees, negotiating and voting on reorganisation plans.
  • Proof-of-claim filings: drafting, submission, verification and challenging unjustified claims.
  • Defence of directors and shareholders against personal liability claims.
  • Recovery of claims and enforcement against the debtor's assets.
  • Assistance for investors interested in acquiring assets or stakes in insolvent companies.

We become your trusted partner in protecting your financial and commercial interests, whichever side you are on: debtor, creditor or investor.
