Art. 16

Insurance Law

Legal assistance in dealings with insurance companies, in cases of non-payment, delays or refusal to pay compensation.

Types of insurance covered

  • Motor third-party liability and CASCO policies
  • Life and health insurance
  • Property insurance
  • Professional and civil-liability insurance

Our services include

  • Review of insurance policies: identifying abusive or unlawful clauses.
  • Assistance with the compensation procedure: notifying claims, preparing documentation, negotiating with the insurer.
  • Court representation to recover damages in cases of unjustified refusal or delay.
  • Advice on choosing policies, tailored to actual risk, and renegotiating contract terms.
