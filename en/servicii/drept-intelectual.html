Art. 13

Intellectual Property Law

Protecting rights over the results of intellectual activity — inventions, copyright, patents and trademarks.

Under the Civil Code of the Republic of Moldova, intellectual property is the right of individuals or legal entities over the results of their intellectual activity, technologies and inventions, as well as over copyright, patents, trademarks and other similar subject matter.

Classification of intellectual property

  • Rights in the industrial and scientific field
  • Copyright in literature, art and other creative works

Our services include

  • Advice on protecting intellectual-property rights
  • Registration and patenting of rights, including internationally (the PCT system)
  • Representation in administrative, judicial and arbitral proceedings
  • Protection against unfair competition, misleading advertising and breach of trade secrets
  • Preparing court actions for infringement of rights
  • Managing the transfer of intellectual-property rights
  • Registration of databases, software and trademarks
  • Assistance protecting know-how, franchising arrangements and trade secrets
