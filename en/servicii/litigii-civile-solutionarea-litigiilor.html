Art. 15

Civil Litigation and Dispute Resolution

Specialist legal representation in all types of litigation, before national and international courts.

Areas covered

  • Civil disputes (material or moral damages, inheritance, property, division of assets, divorce)
  • Commercial disputes (contract non-performance, conflicts between partners, debt recovery)
  • Tax disputes (taxes, duties, tax audits, VAT refunds)
  • Employment disputes (dismissals, wages, damages, discrimination)

Our services include

  • Full court representation: first instance, appeal, cassation, including enforcement; preparing appeals, cassation applications and extraordinary remedies.
  • Alternative dispute resolution: mediation, arbitration and amicable settlements, whenever they best serve the client's interests.
  • Tailored analysis and strategy: case assessment, procedural planning, preparing evidence and expert opinions.
