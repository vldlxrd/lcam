Art. 18

Criminal Law

Thorough analysis of the evidence and all the legal steps needed for a favourable outcome in criminal cases.

Defence and representation throughout the criminal process

  • Representation at every stage — from the criminal investigation, through trial court, appeal and the supreme court of justice
  • Building an effective defence strategy
  • Securing a reduction or annulment of the sentence
  • Protection against abuse or pressure from the prosecuting authorities
  • Challenging inaction or an unjustified refusal to open a criminal investigation

Advice and strategic preparation

  • Advice on criminal procedure and the specific circumstances of the case
  • Legal analysis of the evidence and circumstances
  • Gathering evidence supporting the position of the suspect, defendant, convicted person or victim
  • Full assistance during interrogations, hearings, inquiries and trials, to prevent abuse
  • Preparing the necessary procedural documents
  • Representation before investigative bodies, courts and the prosecutor's office, including a dedicated "personal lawyer" service
