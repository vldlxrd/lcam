Art. 06

Tax Law

Legal and tax consultancy to optimise your tax obligations and to prevent or resolve disputes with the authorities.

What we offer

  • Ongoing tax consultancy for companies and individuals, on the interpretation of the Tax Code of the Republic of Moldova.
  • Tax optimisation of your business, through the right choice of legal form and tax structure.
  • Appeals and complaints submitted to the State Tax Service.
  • Assistance and representation during tax audits, from preparing documentation to drafting objections.
  • Representation in tax disputes — VAT, excise duty, corporate income tax, personal income tax, dividend tax.
  • VAT, excise or tax refunds and offsets, including in cases of unpaid invoices.
  • Tax incentives for foreign investors.
  • Tax defence strategies, particularly where the authorities misapply the law.

Working with us means a personalised, strategic approach to every tax situation, aimed at reducing your financial and legal risk.
