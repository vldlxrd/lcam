Legal Due Diligence

Specialist legal due diligence for companies and investors — essential input for sound decision-making.

Our services include

  • Detailed review of the legal position of a company, project or transaction — ongoing or potential litigation, contractual obligations, ownership rights, tax, employment and data-protection compliance.
  • Assessment of legal risk and its short- and long-term implications.
  • Clear legal reports, with precise conclusions and concrete recommendations.
  • Scope tailored to the client's purpose: investment, reorganisation, sale or another transaction.

Why choose this service

  • You get a complete, accurate legal picture
  • You benefit from practical solutions to legal risks and issues
  • You protect your business ahead of major transactions or decisions
