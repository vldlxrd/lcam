Art. 02

Corporate Law / Mergers and Acquisitions

Complete legal support from the incorporation of a company through to its liquidation or reorganisation.

How we can help, concretely

  • Setting up corporate entities: choosing the right legal form (LLC, JSC, branches, representative offices), drafting and registering constitutive documents, bylaws and internal regulations.
  • Corporate governance: efficient management and internal control structures, compliance policies and conflict-of-interest prevention.
  • Liquidations, bankruptcies and reorganisations: full support in dissolution, voluntary liquidation, insolvency or judicial/extrajudicial reorganisation.
  • Shareholder agreements: pre-emption rights, transfer restrictions, dividend policies.
  • Corporate disputes and litigation: shareholder conflicts, disputes between companies, transactions with conflict potential.
  • Recovery of trade receivables, including enforcement or amicable settlement.
  • Review and negotiation of international contracts and structuring of international sale agreements.
  • Sale and purchase of goods and assets, including public tenders and public procurement.
  • Agency and distribution agreements, local and international.
  • Mergers and acquisitions (M&A): legal due diligence, deal structuring, drafting of share/asset purchase agreements, management of legal and tax risk.

We become your trusted partner at every stage of your business's growth — not just point-in-time legal assistance, but a strategic view of the company's risks and opportunities.
