With a team of lawyers with extensive experience in national and international law, we offer real legal partnerships built on trust, discretion and concrete results.

In an increasingly regulated and competitive economy, we provide ongoing support to clients who want not just to avoid legal problems, but to turn the law into a strategic tool for protection and growth.

We cover a wide range of services: from consultancy and legal audits to court representation, international arbitration, drafting complex contracts, managing relationships with the authorities, criminal defence and commercial litigation.

Our team has over 10 years of practical experience, and our portfolio includes hundreds of successfully resolved cases. Our lawyers are valued not only for their solid legal knowledge, but also for their excellent negotiation and communication skills.

What sets us apart

  • Tailored legal subscriptions (Basis, Standard, Premium) — an entire external legal department, with no internal operating costs.
  • Detailed legal audits, particularly in HR, contracts, taxation, and corporate and real-estate compliance.
  • Financial efficiency — you avoid the need for your own legal department, with access to a constantly updated, multidisciplinary team.
  • Absolute discretion — we strictly observe professional secrecy and protect our clients' data and image.
  • A strong network of partners — notaries, bailiffs, private investigators, accounting experts and appraisers.

Our clients include large and medium-sized companies, entrepreneurs, investors and managers, as well as individuals with specific legal needs. All of them receive the same seriousness, speed of response and total commitment from us.

Legal Corporate Assist doesn't just offer legal services — we offer safety, stability and continuity. We are your trusted partner, however complex your situation may be.
